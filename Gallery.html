Legacy

The Pyramids of Giza endure as UNESCO World Heritage Sites and symbols of ancient Egypt's cultural and architectural legacy.


They have inspired wonder for centuries, and today, they remain among the most visited tourist attractions globally, drawing millions annually to marvel at their timeless splendor.
